Privacy Policy for Stegora

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Stegora ("the App") is developed to help users securely hide files and messages inside PNG and JPG images using steganography and optional AES-256 encryption.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Stegora handles your information.

Information We Collect

Stegora does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal details.

Local Processing

All embedding, extraction, encryption, and decryption operations are performed entirely on your device. Images, files, messages, passwords, and encrypted data never leave your device unless you choose to share them yourself.

Internet Access

Stegora is designed to work completely offline. An internet connection is not required to use the app's core functionality.

Permissions

The App may request permission to access your device's storage or media library so you can select images and save generated files. These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the App and are never used to collect or transmit your personal information.

Data Security

Stegora supports optional AES-256 encryption to protect hidden files and messages with a password. Passwords are used only on your device during encryption and decryption. They are never stored, transmitted, or shared by the App.

Third-Party Services

Stegora does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect personal information.

Children's Privacy

Stegora does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect user data, no personal information from children is stored or processed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised Effective Date.
